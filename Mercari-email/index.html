Shipping price update

Each year, shipping and delivery carriers review their pricing and make rate adjustments to account for rising fuel costs and inflation. Since carrier rates are rising, shipping fees for Mercari prepaid labels are also increasing for certain weights starting February 1, 2023. You don’t need to take any action to update shipping on any existing listings, and you’ll still get an average of 40% savings for items between 0.25 lb – 50 lbs compared to the retail prices of our carriers (USPS, UPS, FedEx)*.

*All fees and rates are based on a comparison completed December 18, 2022.

Make room for you

It’s not about ‘new year, new you,’ but about making room for yourself in the new year, letting go of items that don’t serve you, and being kind to yourself with a clear space and mind. Did you get some gifts that aren’t quite your style, or that you’re unlikely to use? Listing is the new re-gifting – make room for the gifts you’re excited about, find new homes for the rest… and make some money while you’re at it. 🥰
